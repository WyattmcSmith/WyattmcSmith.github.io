About Me

Hello! My Name is Wyatt Smith. I am a Junior here at the Univeristy of South Carolina studying Biology and Chemistry. Outside of my academic life... which in all honesty takes up most of my life I love to be active. My mood is really based around my ability to complete a days work and then be able to get some sun.

A little more about me. I am a Firefighter and EMT here in Columbia. I am stationed of Station 30, otherwise known as the "Dirty Thirty". Outside of my time in EMS I am a huge Rugby fan and use to play here at South Carolina until I had to make the decision between school and sports. I am a huge animal lover and have foster a lot of dogs and a few cats in my life. If I could choose the perfect pet for me it would be an Irish Wolfhound simply because it is bigger than me!

My Future Career Plans

Unforttunately my academic career will not stop with the completion of my undergraduate degree as I am hoping to pursue a career in medicine. Obviously looking at my resume in EMS becoming a ER Doctor is not too hard of a transition. However I hope for the ability to imerse my self into a specialty of medicine. Endocrinology, pulmonology, and cardiology are my top three, but to be completely honest... I will be thankful for any opportunity in medicine I am given.

Outside of my medical school plans I do hope to continue to be able to volunteer as a Firefighter and EMT. I do love my current work but I do fear that I will develop burnout, it is very intense work and can take a lot out of a person. Outside of ER work the possibility of doing pediatric pulmonology is something that is very interesting to me. This is due to my struggles breathing as a young child and how eventually I was able to overcome them. Surgery is another possible route for me as many surgeons in my life have inspired me.
